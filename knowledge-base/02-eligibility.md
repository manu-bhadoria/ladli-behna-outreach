# 02 — Eligibility

Eligibility has two layers: the **original 2023 rules** (still the baseline) and ongoing **disqualification triggers** that remove existing beneficiaries.

## Who is eligible

| Criterion | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Gender | Woman |
| Residence | Permanent resident of Madhya Pradesh |
| Age | 21 to 60 years (lower bound was 23 in the original notification; effective operational floor is 21 for married women) |
| Marital status | Married, widowed, divorced, or abandoned by husband |
| Household income | Family annual income ≤ ₹2,50,000 |
| Bank account | Must hold her own bank account, Aadhaar-linked and DBT-enabled |
| Samagra ID | Must be enrolled in the MP Samagra family database and e-KYC must be complete |

## Who is NOT eligible

A woman is disqualified if **any one** of the following applies to her or any member of her family:

- Family annual income exceeds ₹2.5 lakh.
- Any family member is an income-tax payer.
- Any family member is a permanent/regular government employee, pensioner, or elected representative (MP, MLA, Zilla Panchayat member, etc. — some local-body exceptions apply).
- Family owns 5 acres or more of agricultural land.
- Family owns a 4-wheeler (tractor is excluded from this restriction).
- She is already receiving ≥ ₹1,250 / month under any other government cash-transfer scheme (to prevent double-dipping).

"Family" in this scheme means the unit recorded together in the Samagra database.

## Age-related disqualification (triggers removal)

- When a beneficiary **crosses 60**, her entry is removed from the active list — this is the single largest reason for deletions. Approximately **3.04 lakh women** have been removed for this reason (cumulative through early 2026).
- Death of beneficiary also triggers removal (~15,748 names removed on this ground).

## Unmarried women

Unmarried women are **not** eligible under the baseline rules. Attempts at political pressure to extend to unmarried women have surfaced in various assembly debates but have not resulted in a formal rule change as of the current cycle.

## Quick eligibility flowchart (for field use)

```
Is she a permanent resident of MP?                 → No → INELIGIBLE
Is she 21–60 years old?                            → No → INELIGIBLE
Is she married / widowed / divorced / abandoned?   → No → INELIGIBLE
Family income ≤ ₹2.5L AND no IT payer AND
no 4-wheeler AND < 5 acres AND no govt job in HH?  → No → INELIGIBLE
Aadhaar-linked DBT bank account + Samagra e-KYC?   → No → fix first, then ELIGIBLE
All checks pass                                    → ELIGIBLE
```
