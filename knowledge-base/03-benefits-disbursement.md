# 03 — Benefits & Disbursement

## Current monthly amount

**₹1,500 per month per eligible woman**, credited via DBT.

## Amount over time

| Period | Monthly amount | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| June 2023 – Sept 2023 | ₹1,000 | Scheme launch |
| Oct 2023 onward | ₹1,250 | Pre-election hike by Shivraj Singh Chouhan govt |
| Aug 2024 (one-off) | +₹250 on top | "Rakhi Shagun" bonus for Raksha Bandhan |
| Nov 2024 – present | ₹1,500 | Diwali hike announced by CM Mohan Yadav |
| Target: by 2028 | ₹3,000 | Public commitment by CM Mohan Yadav (election-year target) |

The ₹3,000 figure is a **stated future target**, not a current entitlement. Any outreach claim of "₹3,000 confirmed" without a timeline caveat is incorrect.

## Occasional bonuses

The government has historically topped up the monthly amount around specific festivals:

- **Raksha Bandhan (Rakhi Shagun):** +₹250 as a one-time bonus (done in Aug 2024 with the 27th installment, and signalled as an annual pattern).
- **Diwali / state anniversaries:** Used as timing pegs for permanent amount hikes rather than one-offs.

## Disbursement mechanism

1. State treasury releases the consolidated tranche (typically ₹1,500–₹2,000 crore per month, depending on live beneficiary count).
2. Funds flow to NPCI-mapped Aadhaar-linked bank accounts of beneficiaries.
3. CM presides over a ceremonial "transfer" event at a district HQ on release day — this is the photo-op that local newspapers cover.
4. Beneficiary receives an SMS on her registered mobile once credited.

## What the cash can legally be used for

There is **no restriction** on how the beneficiary spends the money. It is an unconditional cash transfer to her personal bank account. The state's own messaging emphasises nutrition, health, and economic self-reliance, but these are suggested uses, not conditions.
