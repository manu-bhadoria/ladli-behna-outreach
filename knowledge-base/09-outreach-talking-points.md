# 09 — Outreach Talking Points

Pre-drafted messaging for common outreach surfaces. Lift and adapt — do not invent new numbers not supported by files 01–08.

## 30-second pitch (spoken / call-centre)

> मुख्यमंत्री लाड़ली बहना योजना मध्य प्रदेश सरकार की योजना है जिसमें हर पात्र बहन के अपने बैंक खाते में हर महीने ₹1,500 सीधे जमा होते हैं। बहन की उम्र 21 से 60 के बीच होनी चाहिए, वह विवाहित/विधवा/तलाकशुदा/त्यक्ता हो, परिवार की सालाना आय ₹2.5 लाख से कम हो, और उसका समग्र e-KYC और आधार-बैंक लिंक पूरा हो। कोई आवेदन शुल्क नहीं है।

**English equivalent:** Mukhyamantri Ladli Behna Yojana is a Madhya Pradesh government scheme that credits ₹1,500 every month directly to each eligible woman's own bank account. She must be between 21 and 60 years old, married/widowed/divorced/abandoned, family income under ₹2.5 lakh/year, with Samagra e-KYC and Aadhaar-bank linking complete. There is no application fee.

## Opening line (SMS / WhatsApp / print)

> आपके खाते में हर महीने ₹1,500 — मुख्यमंत्री लाड़ली बहना योजना। जानिए पात्रता और आवेदन की जानकारी।

(No hype, no promised amounts that aren't current, no political branding unless the channel explicitly requires it.)

## If asked "how much will I get?"

Current: ₹1,500/month.
Target: ₹3,000/month by 2028 (a government commitment, not today's entitlement).

Do **not** say "₹3,000 guaranteed" or "₹3,000 starting next month."

## If asked "how do I apply?"

1. Go to your nearest Gram Panchayat, Ward office, or announced camp site.
2. Carry Samagra ID, Aadhaar card, bank passbook, mobile number, photo.
3. Fill the application form on the spot.
4. Complete Samagra e-KYC — online at `samagra.gov.in` or via biometric at a ration shop / MP Online kiosk / CSC.
5. Track status at `cmladlibahna.mp.gov.in`.

## If asked "why was my name removed?"

Most common reason is crossing age 60 (hard ceiling). Other reasons: Samagra mismatch, verification re-flag (income/asset/govt job in family), bank seeding issues. **See `07-exclusions-grievances.md` for the full diagnostic walkthrough.**

## Do-not-say list

- ❌ "This scheme covers all women in MP." (It's means-tested at ₹2.5L family income and excludes unmarried women.)
- ❌ "Unmarried women can also apply." (They cannot, under current rules.)
- ❌ "₹3,000 is active now." (Target, not current.)
- ❌ "Payment will come on the 10th every month." (Typical but not guaranteed — installment dates shift.)
- ❌ Any promise that a specific removal case will be reversed. (Outreach can help route a grievance; it cannot promise an outcome.)
- ❌ Any campaign / political framing in an official-government-style outreach channel.

## Tone

- Transactional, specific, fact-heavy. Lead with the number (₹1,500) and the mechanism (DBT to her own bank account).
- Avoid "*behna*" and sisterhood rhetoric in formal grievance or eligibility conversations — save it for celebratory channels only.
- In Hindi, prefer simple, everyday vocabulary over Sanskritised bureaucratic terms (e.g., *खाता* over *लेखा*, *पैसा* over *धनराशि*).

## Channel-specific notes

- **Voice bot / IVR:** Lead with eligibility gate ("kya aap Madhya Pradesh ki nivasi hain?") before anything else — saves airtime on ineligible callers.
- **SMS:** 160-char limit — use the opener above and a link to the portal. Do not include the Rs sign; use "Rs" or "INR" for maximum carrier compatibility.
- **Field flip-card:** Put the three disqualifiers (income > ₹2.5L, 4-wheeler, govt job in family) on the back — this is what field officers need most.
- **Grievance hotline script:** Follow the diagnostic order in file 07 strictly. Do not skip the e-KYC check — it's the #1 silent cause of non-credit.
