## Notes on source quality

- **State press releases & Free Press Journal / Dainik Jagran / India TV** are reliable for installment sizes, dates, and location of CM events.
- **Bajaj Finserv, ClearTax, MyScheme** are reliable for eligibility rules and document lists (they mirror the official notification).
- **Scheme aggregator sites** (sarkarimantra, yojanasarkar, cmv360) are decent for overview but occasionally conflate MP's Ladli Behna with Maharashtra's Ladki Bahin — cross-check any figure that seems off.
- **Cross-check rule:** If only one source makes a claim, flag it as such. Don't elevate single-source claims into KB facts without confirmation.
